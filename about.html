About Us

What we do

Building Impactful Learning Programs
We strive to make quality education and life-long learning more accessible in Papua New Guinea.

upskillingPNG is a joint initiative led by Active Services LTD in partnership with TMTGroup – an education technology company that specializes in the development and delivery of scalable e-learning programs. We are the partner of choice of leading institutions for the implementation of various learning programs such as professional certifications, scholarships and workforce upskilling.

Our Services

Our partners benefit from core services and expertise to efficiently develop and launch their learning programs.

INSTRUCTIONAL DESIGN

Provide instructional design expertise to develop online course resources with partner’s subject matter expert(s).

CONTENT PRODUCTION

Provide content preproduction - postproduction expertise and needed recording facility and equipment for the development of video course contents.

COURSE AUTHORING

Course quality checking, authoring, uploading, hosting, deployment, and maintenance on the coursebank.ph online platform.

DEPLOYMENT SUPPORT

Provide program administration and strategic communication support as well as learner support to ensure successful implementation of programs.
